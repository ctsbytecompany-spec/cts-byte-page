Privacy Policy – Wayzza

Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

Wayzza collects the following data:

  • Location Data: Your GPS coordinates, altitude, speed, and accuracy when you track a route
  • Route Information: Route names, start/end times, and distance traveled
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version (for compatibility purposes only)

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect any personal information like your name, email, or account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To enable route tracking and display on the map
  • To calculate and display route statistics (distance, time, speed)
  • To allow you to export and import your route data
  • To improve app performance and stability

3. Data Storage & Security

Your location and route data are stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local storage (SQLite). We do not send this data to external servers or clouds unless you explicitly export it.

When you export data, it is saved as a JSON file on your device storage. You have full control over where this file goes and who can access it.

4. Permissions We Request

Wayzza requests the following permissions:

  • Location Access (Always): Required to track your GPS position during route recording
  • Background Location: Required to continue tracking when the app runs in the background

These permissions are used only for tracking routes and are necessary for the app to function correctly.

5. Third-Party Services

Wayzza uses MapLibre for displaying maps. MapLibre may collect usage statistics according to its own privacy policy. No personal data is shared with MapLibre beyond what is necessary to display the map.

We do not use analytics trackers, ad networks, or other third-party services that collect your personal data.

6. Data Retention

All route data is retained on your device until you manually delete it. You can delete individual routes or all data at any time through the app's settings.

7. Your Rights & Control

  • You have complete control over your data. You can export, import, or delete routes at any time.
  • You can revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings (note: the app won't function without this permission).
  • You can uninstall the app at any time, which removes all data from your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Wayzza is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
